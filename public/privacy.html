Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2025

Sorvo Studio develops and publishes free mobile applications. This page informs visitors about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone using our apps.

Information Collection and Use

We may collect limited personal information, including device identifiers and in-app behavior data, to provide and improve our services.

The apps may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include but are not limited to:

  • Unity Ads
  • ironSource (LevelPlay)
  • Google AdMob
  • TikTok Ads & Attribution
  • Unity Analytics
  • Unity IAP (In-App Purchasing)
  • Unity Leaderboards (Unity Gaming Services)
  • Facebook SDK

Leaderboard and Gameplay Data

Some apps may offer leaderboards or cloud features that submit gameplay scores and player identifiers to our backend services. This data is only used for gameplay purposes and not shared with third parties outside of the services listed above.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
